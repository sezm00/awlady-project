Our Programs

Activities

At Awlady Orphanage, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing and supportive environment for children who have lost their parents or guardians. Our commitment extends beyond just meeting the basic needs of food, shelter, and clothing. We strive to create a home where every child feels loved, valued, and empowered to reach their full potential.
Our range of activities is carefully designed to address the diverse needs and interests of the children under our care. Through a blend of educational, recreational, and therapeutic programs, we aim to foster holistic development and instill a sense of belonging and purpose in each child.

EDUCATIONAL ENRICHMENT


We believe that education is the key to unlocking a child's future. That's why we offer a variety of educational workshops and tutoring sessions tailored to the individual learning needs of each child. From basic literacy and numeracy skills to advanced subjects, we provide the resources and support necessary to help every child succeed academically.

EDUCATIONAL WORKSHOPS

PROMOTING HEALTH AND WELLNESS


The well-being of our children is our top priority. We offer comprehensive health and wellness programs that encompass physical, emotional, and mental health. Regular check-ups, nutritious meals, and access to counseling services ensure that our children grow up healthy, happy, and resilient.

HEALTH PROGRAMS

CREATIVE EXPRESSION AND ARTS


Creative Expression and Arts Programs: Creativity knows no bounds, and we provide ample opportunities for our children to explore their artistic talents. Whether it's painting, music, dance, or theater, our creative arts programs allow children to express themselves freely and develop confidence in their abilities.

CREATIVE ARTS PROGRAMS

CULTURAL EXCHANGES AND DIVERSITY


Cultural Exchanges and Diversity: We celebrate diversity and encourage cultural exchange within our community. Through cultural events, language classes, and interactions with individuals from different backgrounds, we foster a spirit of understanding, tolerance, and appreciation for other cultures.

CULTURAL EXCHANGES

Achievments

Empowering Over 300 Children


Awlady Orphanage has provided a nurturing home for over 300 boys and girls, ranging from newborns to adults. Through a family-like atmosphere, each child receives personalized care and support tailored to their needs.

Holistic Growth and Development


The orphanage prioritizes the physical, mental, and emotional well-being of every child, fostering holistic growth and development in a safe and nurturing environment.

Emphasis on Education


With a strong emphasis on education, Awlady Orphanage ensures that each child has access to quality schooling and educational resources. This commitment to education lays the foundation for future success and empowerment.

Vocational Training Programs


In addition to academic education, the orphanage provides vocational training programs tailored to the interests and abilities of the children. These programs equip them with valuable skills and knowledge, preparing them for meaningful employment and self-sufficiency in the future.

Promoting Productive Citizenship


By focusing on education and vocational training, Awlady Orphanage empowers children to become productive members of society. Through nurturing their talents and abilities, the orphanage instills a sense of responsibility and citizenship, fostering a brighter future for both the children and the community.
