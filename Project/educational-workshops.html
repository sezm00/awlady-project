Educational Workshops

At Awlady ("My Children") Orphanage, we believe that education is the key to unlocking a brighter future for our children. Through our Educational Workshops program, we provide a supportive and stimulating environment where children can enhance their academic skills, expand their knowledge, and unlock their potential.


Supplementary Classes to Reinforce Public Education


Our Educational Workshops offer supplementary classes designed to reinforce and complement the public education system. Whether it's providing additional support in core subjects like mathematics, science, and language arts, or offering enrichment classes in areas such as history, geography, and literature, we ensure that every child has the opportunity to excel academically and reach their full potential.

Basic Computer Training


In today's digital age, computer literacy is essential for success in school and beyond. That's why we offer basic computer training workshops to equip our children with the skills they need to navigate the digital world with confidence. From learning how to use word processing software and internet browsers to understanding basic coding concepts, our computer training workshops empower children to harness the power of technology for learning and communication.

Hands-On Learning and Interactive Activities


Our Educational Workshops go beyond traditional classroom instruction to offer hands-on learning experiences and interactive activities. Whether it's conducting science experiments, exploring historical artifacts, or participating in group discussions and debates, children are actively engaged in the learning process, fostering curiosity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

Life-Long Learning and Personal Development


At Awlady Orphanage, we believe that education is not just about acquiring knowledge; it's also about personal growth and development. Through our Educational Workshops, children develop essential life skills such as time management, organization, and communication, setting them on the path to success in school, career, and life.

Join Us in Empowering Young Minds

We invite you to join us in empowering young minds through our Educational Workshops at Awlady Orphanage. Through donating educational resources, or sponsoring a workshop, your support helps us provide quality education and opportunities for children in need. Together, let's unlock the potential of every child and build a brighter future for generations to come.
