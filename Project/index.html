OUR MISSION


Since its establishment in [insert founding year], Awlady Orphanage has been dedicated to supporting orphaned children in Egypt.

We believe in providing comprehensive care to orphaned children, addressing both their material needs through financial assistance
and their emotional needs through the dedication of in-house volunteers. We are firm believers in the transformative power of support for children. Given the right resources, we believe they can shape the future for the better.

HOW YOU CAN MAKE A DIFFERENCE


Every child deserves love and support for their healthy development. By volunteering with Awlady Orphanage in Al Maadi, you offer invaluable emotional support.
