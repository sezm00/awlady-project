Health and Wellness Programs

At Awlady ("My Children") Orphanage, we prioritize the physical and mental health of every child under our care. Through our Health Programs, we provide comprehensive services and support to promote overall well-being and empower children to lead healthy and fulfilling lives.


Regular Medical Check-ups and Preventive Care


Our Health Programs begin with regular medical check-ups and preventive care measures to monitor the health status of each child and address any concerns proactively. From routine vaccinations and screenings to dental and vision check-ups, we ensure that children receive the essential medical care they need to stay healthy and thrive.

Nutritious Meals and Dietary Education


Healthy eating habits are fundamental to overall health and well-being. That's why we prioritize nutritious meals and dietary education as part of our Health Programs. Our dedicated kitchen staff prepares balanced and wholesome meals that meet the nutritional needs of growing children, while educational workshops and cooking classes teach children about the importance of healthy eating and how to make nutritious choices.

Physical Fitness and Recreation


Physical activity is not only essential for physical health but also for mental and emotional well-being. Our Health Programs include opportunities for children to engage in physical fitness activities and recreation, such as sports, yoga, dance, and outdoor play. These activities promote physical strength, coordination, and cardiovascular health, as well as stress relief and relaxation.

Promoting Hygiene and Personal Care


Good hygiene practices are essential for preventing illness and maintaining overall health. Our Health Programs include education and training on personal hygiene practices such as handwashing, dental care, and sanitation. By promoting good hygiene habits, we empower children to take responsibility for their health and well-being.

Join Us in Prioritizing Health and Well-being


We invite you to join us in prioritizing health and well-being at Awlady Orphanage. Through donating medical supplies, or supporting our Health Programs financially, your involvement helps us provide essential healthcare services and support to children in need. Together, let's ensure that every child has the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential.
